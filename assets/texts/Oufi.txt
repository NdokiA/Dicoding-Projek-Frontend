Öufi Association specializes in managing contracts, deploying Fixers to oversee an agreement between parties and ensure their completion upon request. The contracts go into effect as soon as they are submitted while breach of contract will result in the execution of involved parties.

Öufi Association will continuously send Association Fixers to execute the violator. 

From the Öufi Association IDs, I learned that they fight with halberds bearing rectangular blades, designed to immediately amputating and stagger the enemies.
