Dieci Association specializes in collection and research of knowledge. They possess enromous libraries for their Fixers to gain knowledge. They also run an orphanages, taking children and raising them to join the Association.

Dieci Assosciate Fixers are divided into the Fists and the Keys based on the method of acquiring knowledge. The Fists "directly grasping it with their own hands" while the Keys used the tools to study it indirectly. 

Judging from the way Dieci Fixer IDs fight, they're seemingly convert their knowledge into strength by enhancing their physical power (the Fists) or their weapon (the Keys). The more precious the knowledge, the greater the strength would be. The drawback, however, once they draw their strength, they'd lose their knowledge
