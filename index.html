Associations

Associations seems to refer to large organizations that manage fixers. There are twelve Associations in the city. Each with their own expertises

They're seemingly acts as the major collective that manages the fixers. They work at much greater scale than your typical Fixer Office. Associations are divided into Branches, further divided into several Sections.

Also, each association abides on their own structure, uniforms, and rules. We've met some associate fixers during our journey, and some sinner IDs gave me some informations to fill me in.
